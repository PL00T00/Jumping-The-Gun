<h1> IDEA! </h1>

A basic 2D platformer where you are a robot with a gun. You can't jump (cause you have wheels) but your gun has crazy recoil and pushes you back. There are no enemies, the gun is only for movement. 

You get gun upgrades at every check point that help you, this could include:
- Gun starts as pistol, upgrades to assualt rifle to shoot more bullets.
- Secondary "shotgun" shot that takes longer to reload but has more recoil.
- Flashlight to see in the dark.
- Grappling hook type shot on your gun.
- Laser scope thingy that shows where you are pointing.

Game has a dark Wall-E type aestetich, deep oranges and dark colours.
Real world locations like abandonned apartment buildings to explore.
Goal is to get to space and leave the abandonned Earth, same message as Wall-E XD
